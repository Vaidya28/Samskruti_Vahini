Education First

Our foundation is led by educationists with a deep belief in strong foundational learning for society. We run weekend STEM workshops, teacher training, and plan free coaching centres & open experimental labs.

Community & Care

We organise medical awareness sessions on gynaecological and other issues, self-defence programs for girls, and provide financial aid like college admission and tuition fees to deserving students.

Technology for Impact

Through an MoU with a women’s engineering college, student volunteers build real apps—school ERP, surveys, e-commerce, and outreach—to bridge CSR intent with on-ground outcomes.

What we do

Clear, outcome-driven initiatives designed for Tier-3 realities.

Student Workshops (STEM)

  • Interdisciplinary STEM learning & organic farming modules.
  • Weekend format to suit school schedules.
  • SAIL-CSR supported workshops in Durgapur.

Health & Self-Defence

  • Medical awareness for girls & women on gynaecological and other issues.
  • Self-defence workshops for confidence & safety.

Teacher Training

  • Activity-based, interdisciplinary methods per NEP.
  • Google Forms for assignments & data collection.
  • Enable low-budget schools to compete with corporate schools.

Financial Support (Education)

  • Admission fee & tuition fee support for underprivileged students.
  • Focus on girls and economically weaker sections.

Skill Development & Training

  • Handicrafts and Indian traditional food-making.
  • Direct sales of products made by women trainees.
  • E-commerce storefront launching on this site.

Technology & CSR

  • MoU with women’s engineering college for volunteer internships.
  • Student-built apps: School ERP, surveys, e-commerce & outreach.
  • Bridge CSR inputs with trackable learning outcomes.
